What is STRK20

A Starknet-native privacy layer for ERC-20 tokens. Private balances and transfers live inside one canonical multi-asset pool.

One pool, all tokens

A single multi-asset privacy pool handles any ERC-20 on Starknet. No fragmented liquidity, no per-token pools.

Note discovery

Wallets scan a user’s own channels instead of the entire pool. Finding your funds is fast without compromising privacy.

Anonymous DeFi

Open notes make anonymous swaps and staking practical against existing Starknet liquidity. Who traded is hidden – not the trade itself.

Selective disclosure

Encrypted viewing keys let an authorized entity audit a specific user’s history without affecting anyone else. A path for regulated flows.

Same account model

Privacy fits inside the existing Starknet account and app ecosystem. No second wallet, no separate chain, no migration.

Built on Cairo + STARKs

Cairo-based proving, no trusted setup, in-protocol proof verification, native account abstraction. Privacy that is native to Starknet, not bolted on.

The honest trade

This is practical privacy, not maximalist privacy. Deposits and withdrawals are public. Anonymous AMM swaps hide who traded, not the trade amounts. Selective disclosure adds an authorized audit path for regulated use.
